We are equal parts dreamers and builders.

Square One Eco Africa is driving transformative sustainability in Sub-Saharan Africa by addressing environmental and socio-economic challenges through innovative projects, gender inclusivity, and storytelling. Join us to reduce poverty, create green jobs, empower communities, and build resilience.

Transforming Landscapes, Empowering Lives.

We are committed to ecosystem restoration, sustainable agriculture, water access, and green jobs. We build a future with humans and nature living together by reforesting, ecologically documenting, and regenerating solutions.
